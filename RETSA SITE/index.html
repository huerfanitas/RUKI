ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Agustín Pérez Burgos also known as Retsa, a game developer and programmer from Argentina.

As an active member of the underground indie game scene since 2009, i've been part of countless game jams & made a lot of small experimental games.

I also have a youtube channel in spanish talking about the hidden gems in the scene, as well as devlogs of my own games.

Other interest include: creative coding, music, collapse technology, resilence and permaculture.
